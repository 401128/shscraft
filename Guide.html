Lag Fix Guide

Press the ` key on your keyboard, then click Options, then Video Settings.

Change your settings to these:

Render Distance, Max Framerate, FXAA Antialiasing, and Fog are the most important. DO NOT SET YOUR RENDER DISTANCE TO ANYTHING HIGHER THAN 2.

Refer back to this guide if your game starts lagging again. The settings should only be reset if you have to retype your username when opening the site.

These computers have incredibly poor CPUs, 2GB of RAM, and lack a GPU. It's a miracle that Minecraft, even an older optimized version, runs at all on these devices.

Reducing the render distance to 1 greatly improves performance. Fog and FXAA Antialiasing are GPU-intensive, so disabling them also boosts performance. Setting Max Framerate to unlimited increases FPS because Minecraft doesn't cap the framerate when this option is set.
