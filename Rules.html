Rules

No Griefing. (max 24h ban.)

No destroying spawn & killing players at spawn. (max 12h ban.)

Keep the chat somewhat respectful. (max 12h mute.)

No hacked clients/cheating. (minimum 24h ban, harsher punishment for subsequent bans.)

No xraying. (24h ban and your inventory will be cleared.)

No stealing from other players. (max 12h ban, and the stolen items will be returned to the player you stole from.)

Don't play on the server if you're in class and have schoolwork to do. (max 1s ban.)
